About Us

The Velvet Bean Story


Velvet Bean began with a simple idea — to make every cup of coffee feel like a luxury. We searched the world for the smoothest beans, partnering with ethical farms that share our love for quality. Our roasting process is slow and deliberate, designed to draw out deep flavors and silky textures. Every decision, from sourcing to packaging, reflects our dedication to craft and care. But Velvet Bean is more than a name — it's a promise. A promise of richness, comfort, and unforgettable flavor. We believe coffee should be savored, not rushed. That's why we do things differently. We don't just roast beans — we tell stories. And each cup is the beginning of yours.
